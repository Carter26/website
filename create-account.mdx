GS Advertising & Connections offers two account types: a **Business account** for local companies that want to sponsor youth and high school sports teams, and a **Team account** for coaches and organizations looking for funding. Both account types are free to create. Choose the tab below that matches your role and follow the steps to get started.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Business Account">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Register your business">
        Navigate to the sign-up page and select **Business** as your account type. Enter your email address and create a secure password to create your login credentials.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Complete your business profile">
        After registration, fill in the following fields to build a profile that attracts team applications:

        - **Business Name** — your official business name as you want it displayed
        - **Category** — select the option that best describes your business: *Restaurant, Auto Shop, Gym, Insurance, Real Estate, Medical, Retail,* or *Other*
        - **Location** — enter your city and state so nearby teams can find you
        - **Description** — write a brief overview of your business, your values, and why you support local sports
        - **Website** — add your business URL so teams can learn more about you
        - **Phone** — provide a contact number for direct communication
        - **Budget Range** — indicate the general range you're willing to invest in sponsorships
      </Step>
      <Step title="Subscribe and activate your account">
        To post sponsorship listings and receive applications, select a subscription plan — **$20/month** (standard) or **$30/month** (Premium) — and complete payment via **PayPal** or **Cash App**. Submit your payment confirmation through the platform. Your account goes live within **24 hours**.
      </Step>
    </Steps>

    **Profile tips for businesses:**
    - Use a clear, professional profile photo or logo so teams immediately recognize your brand.
    - Be specific in your description about the types of teams or sports you're most interested in supporting.
    - Keep your budget range and contact details up to date to avoid missed opportunities.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Team Account">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Register your team">
        Navigate to the sign-up page and select **Team** as your account type. Enter your email address and create a secure password to create your login credentials.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Complete your team profile">
        After registration, fill in the following fields to build a profile that stands out to sponsors:

        - **Team Name** — your official team or organization name
        - **Sport** — select your sport: *Baseball, Basketball, Cheer, Football, Golf, Hockey, Lacrosse, Soccer, Softball, Swimming, Tennis, Track & Field, Volleyball, Wrestling,* or *Other*
        - **Age Group** — choose the level that applies: *8U, 10U, 12U, 14U, 16U, 18U, High School, College,* or *Adult*
        - **City** — the city where your team is based
        - **State** — the state where your team is based
        - **Athlete Count** — the total number of athletes on your roster
        - **Team Bio** — tell your story: your history, achievements, community involvement, and what makes your team worth investing in
      </Step>
      <Step title="Start browsing and applying">
        Your team account is active immediately — no subscription required. Head to the marketplace to browse sponsorship listings, save opportunities to your dashboard, and submit applications to businesses that are a good fit for your team.
      </Step>
    </Steps>

    **Profile tips for teams:**
    - Include specific accomplishments (league titles, tournament placements) in your team bio to demonstrate credibility.
    - Update your athlete count each season to reflect your current roster.
    - Add a team photo to personalize your profile and help businesses connect with your athletes.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Logging In

Once your account is created, log in at any time by visiting [gsadvertisingandconnections.bolt.host/login](https://gsadvertisingandconnections.bolt.host/login). Enter the email address and password you registered with to access your dashboard. From there, you can manage your profile, listings or applications, and all platform activity.

<Tip>
  Profiles that are fully filled out — with a photo, complete description, and accurate details — receive significantly more applications (for businesses) and faster responses (for teams). Take five extra minutes to complete every field before you start browsing or posting.
</Tip>
