The GS Advertising & Connections marketplace is where local businesses post active sponsorship opportunities for teams like yours. Every listing includes full details about the sponsorship type, available spots, and what the business is looking for — so you can quickly identify opportunities worth applying to and skip the ones that aren't a good fit.

## Access the marketplace

Log in to your team account and navigate to the **Marketplace** page. You'll see all active sponsorship listings from businesses with current subscriptions. Use the filters at the top of the page to narrow results to the opportunities most relevant to your team.

## Browsing tools

The marketplace includes several filters to help you surface the right opportunities quickly:

- **Business category** — filter by the type of business: Restaurant, Auto Shop, Gym, Insurance, Real Estate, Medical, Retail, or Other
- **Location** — narrow results to businesses in your city, region, or state to find sponsors already serving your community
- **Sponsorship type** — show only the types of sponsorships that match your team's needs
- **Budget** — filter listings by sponsorship amount to focus on opportunities within a useful range for your team

<Tip>
  Filter by location first. Local businesses that already operate in your community are far more likely to say yes — they already have a stake in supporting local athletes.
</Tip>

## What each listing shows

Click any listing to view its full details before deciding to apply. Each listing includes:

- **Sponsorship type** — the category of sponsorship being offered
- **Amount** — the dollar value of the sponsorship
- **Available spots** — how many teams the business is accepting for this opportunity
- **Description** — the business's overview of what they're offering and what they're looking for
- **Benefits** — what the sponsoring business expects in return (logo placement, social posts, event presence, etc.)
- **Application requirements** — any specific information the business wants you to include in your application

## Sponsorship types

Businesses on the platform can offer the following types of sponsorships:

- **Jersey Sponsorship** — your team wears the business's logo on your uniforms
- **Banner Sponsorship** — the business's banner is displayed at your games or events
- **Tournament Sponsorship** — support for a specific tournament your team is participating in
- **Social Media Promotion** — you promote the business across your team's social media channels
- **Community Event Sponsorship** — joint sponsorship tied to a community event or fundraiser
- **Custom Sponsorship** — a one-of-a-kind arrangement defined by the business

## Saving listings

When you find a listing you want to revisit, click **Save** on the listing card. Saved listings appear in the **Saved Opportunities** section of your dashboard so you can come back to them without searching again.

<Note>
  Only listings from businesses with active subscriptions appear in the marketplace. The number of available spots is displayed on each listing — when spots are filled, the listing is no longer open for new applications.
</Note>
