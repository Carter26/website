## What's included — always free

Every team account on GS Advertising & Connections includes the full feature set at no charge. There are no tiers, trials, or hidden fees.

- Browse the entire sponsorship marketplace
- Apply to unlimited sponsorship opportunities
- Save listings to review later
- Team profile page visible to businesses browsing for local teams
- Application status tracking — see whether each application is pending, accepted, declined, or saved
- Email notifications whenever your application status changes

## Your team profile

Your team profile is the first thing a business sees when reviewing your application. A complete, accurate profile builds credibility and helps local businesses find teams in their community.

Include the following to make your profile as strong as possible:

- **Team name, sport, and age group** — be specific so businesses know exactly who you are
- **City and state** — location is one of the primary ways businesses search for local teams to sponsor
- **Athlete count** — businesses want to know how many athletes their sponsorship supports
- **A compelling team bio** — tell your story, including your coaches, community ties, and what your season looks like

<Tip>
  A detailed bio that explains your team's background, goals, and community impact dramatically increases your acceptance rate. Businesses are far more likely to sponsor a team they feel connected to.
</Tip>
