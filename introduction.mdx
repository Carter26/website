## Quick Start

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create your free account">
    Sign up as a business or team at [gsadvertisingandconnections.bolt.host/register](https://gsadvertisingandconnections.bolt.host/register). Choose your account type — Business or Team — and complete your profile.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Businesses: activate your subscription">
    Business accounts require an active subscription ($20/month) to publish listings. Send payment via PayPal or Cash App, then submit your confirmation to activate within 24 hours.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Post or browse opportunities">
    Businesses create detailed sponsorship listings. Teams browse the marketplace, filter by location and sport, and apply to listings that fit their needs.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Form the partnership">
    Once a business accepts a team's application, both parties receive contact details and begin coordinating the sponsorship details directly.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Who Uses GS Advertising & Connections

| Account Type | Cost | What You Can Do |
|---|---|---|
| **Team** | Free forever | Browse marketplace, apply to listings, save opportunities, track application status |
| **Business (Standard)** | $20/month | Create listings, manage applications, view analytics, contact teams |
| **Business (Premium)** | $30/month | All standard features + priority placement, homepage feature, Premium badge |

<Note>
  Team accounts are always free — there is no cost for youth or high school athletic programs to join, browse, or apply for sponsorships.
</Note>
