Premium Membership is an optional upgrade for businesses that want to stand out in the GS Advertising & Connections marketplace and signal their commitment to the local sports community. At $30/month — just $10 more than the standard plan — Premium gives your business elevated visibility, dedicated recognition, and early access to everything new on the platform. If you want to attract more teams and build your reputation as a community-first sponsor, Premium is worth every dollar.

## How to Upgrade to Premium

<Steps>
  <Step title="Send your payment">
    Send **$30** to one of the two accepted payment methods:

    - **PayPal:** [paypal.me/gsadvertising](https://paypal.me/gsadvertising)
    - **Cash App:** $GSAdvCon
  </Step>
  <Step title="Include your business details in the notes">
    In the payment notes field, include your **business name** and the word **"Premium"** so the team can identify and process your upgrade correctly.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit your payment confirmation">
    After sending payment, visit the [payment confirmation page](/businesses/subscription) and submit the confirmation form with your transaction details.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Get activated within 24 hours">
    GS Advertising & Connections manually verifies all payments. Your Premium Membership will be activated within **24 hours** of confirmed receipt. You'll see your badge and upgraded placement as soon as activation is complete.
  </Step>
</Steps>
