The GS Advertising & Connections marketplace is the central hub of the platform — the place where local businesses and youth sports teams come together to build real community partnerships. Businesses post sponsorship listings that teams can discover and apply to, all in one organized, searchable space.

## How the Marketplace Works

The marketplace displays active sponsorship listings from every business holding an active subscription. When a business's subscription is current, all of its listings appear publicly and are visible to any team browsing the platform. When a subscription expires, those listings are automatically hidden until the business renews — no listings are ever deleted, just temporarily out of view.

Teams access the marketplace completely free of charge. You can browse every active listing, apply filters to narrow your search, and submit applications without paying anything.

## For Businesses

<Steps>
  <Step title="Activate your subscription">
    Your listings only appear in the marketplace once your subscription is active. A standard subscription costs $20/month. See [Subscription & Payments](/businesses/subscription) for setup details.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create your listings">
    Add one or more sponsorship listings to your profile. Each listing describes the opportunity, the benefits you offer, and what you're looking for in a team partner.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Stay visible">
    Keep your subscription current to keep your listings live. If your subscription lapses, renew at any time and your listings are automatically restored.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## For Teams

<Steps>
  <Step title="Browse for free">
    Team accounts are always free. Open the marketplace and start browsing active listings from businesses in your area.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Filter by what matters">
    Use filters to narrow results by location, sport, business category, or listing type so you surface the opportunities most relevant to your team.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Apply directly">
    Find a listing that fits your team and click Apply. Share your team story, explain how the funds will be used, and describe what the business receives in return.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Sponsorship Listing Types

Businesses can post listings across six sponsorship categories, giving teams a wide variety of partnership opportunities to pursue:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Jersey Sponsorship" icon="shirt">
    Your business logo or name appears on a team's game jerseys, putting your brand in front of every fan at every game.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Banner Sponsorship" icon="flag">
    A physical banner featuring your business is displayed at the team's home venue throughout the season.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Tournament Sponsorship" icon="trophy">
    Sponsor a tournament or multi-game event for concentrated visibility with players, families, and spectators.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Social Media Promotion" icon="share-nodes">
    The team promotes your business across their social media channels, expanding your reach to their online audience.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Community Event Sponsorship" icon="people-group">
    Align your brand with local community events — fundraisers, banquets, end-of-season celebrations, and more.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Custom Sponsorship" icon="pen-to-square">
    Work out a unique arrangement that fits your goals and the team's needs — the terms are whatever you both agree on.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Tip>
  Businesses with multiple diverse listing types attract the most teams — a business offering both a jersey sponsorship and a social media promotion gives teams more ways to connect with you. Teams should filter by location first to find the best local matches and build genuine community partnerships.
</Tip>
