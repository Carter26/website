Got questions about how GS Advertising & Connections works? You're in the right place. The answers below cover the most common topics — from signing up and managing your account to sending payments and closing a sponsorship deal. If you don't find what you're looking for here, reach out through the [contact page](https://gsadvertisingandconnections.bolt.host/contact) and the team will get back to you within 24–48 hours.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Is there a cost for teams to join?">
    No. Team accounts are always completely free. Once you register, you can browse every active sponsorship listing in the marketplace, apply to unlimited opportunities, save listings you want to revisit, and track the status of every application you've submitted — all at no cost, with no subscription required.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I activate my business listing?">
    Send your subscription payment to one of the two accepted methods:

    - **PayPal:** [paypal.me/gsadvertising](https://paypal.me/gsadvertising)
    - **Cash App:** $GSAdvCon

    Use your **business name** in the payment notes so the team can match your payment to your account. Then visit the [payment confirmation page](/businesses/subscription) and submit the confirmation form with your transaction details. Your account will be reviewed and activated within **24 hours**.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How long does a business subscription last?">
    Each subscription provides **30 days of access** from the date your account is activated. You can renew at any time — even before your current period ends — to keep your listings live without any gap in visibility.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What happens when my subscription expires?">
    When your subscription period ends, your listings are automatically **hidden** from the public marketplace. Importantly, they are **not deleted**. All your listing content is preserved exactly as you left it. Once you renew your subscription, every listing is instantly restored and becomes visible to teams again — no rebuilding required.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What is the Premium Membership?">
    Premium Membership is a $30/month upgrade (versus $20/month for standard) that gives your business elevated presence across the platform. With Premium you receive:

    - A **Premium Member Badge** displayed on your business profile
    - **Priority placement** in marketplace search results
    - **Featured placement** on the GS Advertising & Connections homepage
    - A **dedicated recognition page** highlighting your support for youth sports
    - **Premium Recognition Status** throughout the platform
    - **First access** to new platform features as they launch

    All standard business features are included. See the [Premium Membership](/platform/premium-membership) page for full details and upgrade instructions.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How are payments processed?">
    GS Advertising & Connections accepts payments through two methods:

    - **PayPal:** [paypal.me/gsadvertising](https://paypal.me/gsadvertising)
    - **Cash App:** $GSAdvCon

    After you send payment, you must submit a **payment confirmation form** on the [payment page](/businesses/subscription) with your business name, payment method, and transaction details. GS Advertising & Connections manually verifies every payment and activates subscriptions within **24 hours** of confirmed receipt. Note that refunds are not offered once a subscription has been activated.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can a business have multiple listings?">
    Yes. Any business with an active subscription can create **unlimited sponsorship listings** across any combination of listing types — Jersey Sponsorship, Banner Sponsorship, Tournament Sponsorship, Social Media Promotion, Community Event Sponsorship, or Custom Sponsorship. Adding multiple diverse listing types is one of the best ways to attract a wider range of teams.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do teams apply for sponsorships?">
    Applying is straightforward:

    1. Browse the marketplace and find a listing that fits your team.
    2. Click **Apply** on the listing.
    3. Fill in your team story, explain how the sponsorship funds will be used, and describe what the business receives in return (logo placement, social media posts, banner display, etc.).
    4. Submit your application.

    The business receives your application and can **accept**, **decline**, or **save** it for later review.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What happens after a team's application is accepted?">
    Once a business accepts an application, **both parties receive each other's contact information**. From there, the business and team coordinate the sponsorship details directly — this includes working out logistics like logo file formats and placement, banner sizing and delivery, social media post timing, event schedules, and any other specifics of the arrangement. GS Advertising & Connections facilitates the connection; the partnership itself is between you and the sponsor.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I contact support?">
    You can reach the GS Advertising & Connections team in two ways:

    - **Email:** [gsadvertisingandconnections@gmail.com](mailto:gsadvertisingandconnections@gmail.com)
    - **Contact form:** [gsadvertisingandconnections.bolt.host/contact](https://gsadvertisingandconnections.bolt.host/contact)

    The typical response time is **within 24–48 hours**. For the fastest response, include your account email and a clear description of your question or issue.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
