When teams discover your listings on the marketplace, they submit applications directly through the platform. Your Applications tab keeps every submission organized so you can evaluate teams at your own pace, act quickly on the best fits, and keep your listings moving toward the right partnerships.

## Where to find your applications

All incoming applications are collected in your dashboard. Navigate to **Dashboard → Applications tab** to see every application submitted across all of your active listings.

## Application statuses

Each application carries a status that reflects where it is in your review process:

- **Pending** — A new application that you haven't reviewed yet. New submissions arrive here by default.
- **Accepted** — You approved this application. Both you and the team receive each other's contact information to begin coordinating the sponsorship.
- **Declined** — You chose not to move forward with this application. The team is notified, and your listing remains open for other applicants.
- **Saved** — You flagged this application to revisit later. Saving does not commit you to any decision.

## Review an application

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Applications tab">
    Go to your **Dashboard** and click the **Applications** tab. You'll see a list of all submissions, filterable by status so you can focus on pending reviews first.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Read the team's profile and message">
    Click on an application to open the full detail view. Review the team's public profile, their bio, and the personal message they wrote for your listing. This context helps you assess whether the team is a strong match for your sponsorship goals.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check remaining spots">
    Confirm how many open spots remain on the listing before making your decision. Once all spots are filled, the listing closes automatically.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Take action">
    Choose one of the three available actions:

    - **Accept** — Approve the team as a sponsorship partner. Both parties immediately receive each other's contact information.
    - **Decline** — Pass on this application. The team is notified, and the spot stays open for future applicants.
    - **Save for later** — Move the application to your Saved queue so you can return to it after reviewing other submissions.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## After you accept

Once you accept an application, GS Advertising & Connections shares your contact information with the team and shares the team's contact information with you. Use those details to reach out directly and work out the logistics of your sponsorship arrangement — payment timing, delivery of materials, posting schedules, or whatever the agreement requires.

<Tip>
  Respond to applications as promptly as you can. Teams often apply to multiple listings at once, and businesses that reply quickly are more likely to secure the partners they want. A reputation for fast, professional responses also encourages more high-quality teams to apply to your future listings.
</Tip>

<Note>
  Saving an application doesn't lock you into anything. Saved applications remain fully actionable — you can accept or decline them at any time, just as you would a pending application.
</Note>
