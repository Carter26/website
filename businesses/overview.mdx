## What you get

Every active business subscription includes the following features:

- **Full Marketplace Visibility** — Your listings appear to all teams browsing the marketplace, giving you broad reach across local sports communities.
- **Unlimited Sponsorship Listings** — Create as many listings as you need across any sponsorship type, with no cap on the number of active opportunities.
- **Team Application Management** — Accept, decline, or save team applications directly from your dashboard to keep your review process organized.
- **Analytics Dashboard** — Track profile views, listing views, and the number of applications you've received to understand how your listings are performing.
- **Business Profile Page** — Your public-facing profile lets teams learn about your business before they apply, building trust and encouraging quality applications.
- **Direct Team Contact** — Once you accept an application, you and the team receive each other's contact information to coordinate sponsorship details offline.
- **Email Notifications** — Stay informed with automatic email alerts whenever a team submits an application to one of your listings.

## Your dashboard

Your dashboard is organized into four tabs, each focused on a different part of managing your presence on the platform:

- **Overview** — A summary of your account activity, including recent applications, listing performance at a glance, and key metrics from your analytics.
- **Listings** — View, edit, and manage all of your sponsorship listings. Create new listings or check the status of existing ones from this tab.
- **Applications** — Review every application teams have submitted to your listings. Filter by status — pending, accepted, declined, or saved — to stay on top of your pipeline.
- **Subscription** — Check your current plan, view your activation and expiration dates, and access payment information to renew or upgrade your membership.

<Tip>
  Keep your listings active and up to date, and aim to respond to new applications within a few days. Businesses that engage quickly build stronger reputations on the platform and consistently receive more applications from high-quality teams.
</Tip>
