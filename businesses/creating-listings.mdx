Your sponsorship listings are how teams find and connect with your business on the marketplace. A clear, detailed listing with a well-defined offer and straightforward application requirements gives teams the confidence to apply — and helps you attract partners that are the right fit for your brand.

**Prerequisite:** You must have an active subscription before you can publish listings. Visit the [Subscription](/businesses/subscription) page to choose a plan and activate your account.

## Create a listing

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the New Listing form">
    Go to your dashboard and click **New Listing**. This opens the listing creation form where you'll configure every detail of your sponsorship offer.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose a sponsorship type">
    Select the type of sponsorship you want to offer. The available types are:

    - **Jersey Sponsorship**
    - **Banner Sponsorship**
    - **Tournament Sponsorship**
    - **Social Media Promotion**
    - **Community Event Sponsorship**
    - **Custom Sponsorship**

    See [Listing types explained](#listing-types-explained) below for a description of each option.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set the sponsorship amount">
    Enter the dollar amount you are offering for this sponsorship. Be specific — teams use this figure to evaluate whether the opportunity fits their fundraising and budget goals.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Describe the listing">
    Write a clear description of what the sponsoring team will receive and what you expect in return. Include details like logo placement, event presence, social post frequency, or any other deliverables so teams know exactly what the arrangement involves.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set available spots">
    Enter the number of teams you are willing to accept for this listing. Once that number of applications has been accepted, the listing closes to additional applicants.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add application requirements">
    Specify what you want teams to include in their application message. For example, you might ask for team size, season schedule, social media handles, or a brief description of the team's community involvement.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Publish the listing">
    Review all your details, then click **Publish**. Your listing will appear immediately on the marketplace and become visible to teams browsing for sponsorship opportunities.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Listing types explained

Choose the sponsorship type that best matches how you want your business to be represented.

- **Jersey Sponsorship** — Your business logo is printed or embroidered on the team's jerseys, giving your brand visibility at every game, practice, and event throughout the season.
- **Banner Sponsorship** — The team displays a banner featuring your business at their games and events, putting your brand in front of players, families, and spectators.
- **Tournament Sponsorship** — Your business sponsors an entire tournament, gaining prominent recognition across all participating teams, venues, and event materials.
- **Social Media Promotion** — The team promotes your business on their social media channels through posts, stories, or shoutouts, extending your reach to their online audience.
- **Community Event Sponsorship** — Your business sponsors a community sports event, associating your brand with a positive local experience and reaching a broad neighborhood audience.
- **Custom Sponsorship** — Define your own unique sponsorship arrangement. Use this type when your offer doesn't fit neatly into one of the categories above.

<Tip>
  Businesses that offer multiple listing types consistently receive the highest volume of applications. Publishing a mix of sponsorship opportunities — such as a jersey listing alongside a social media promotion — gives more teams a way to partner with you and broadens your community reach.
</Tip>

<Note>
  If your subscription expires, your listings are automatically hidden from the marketplace — but they are not deleted. All your listing data is preserved, and your listings restore immediately when you renew your subscription.
</Note>
