## Payment methods

GS Advertising & Connections accepts payment through two platforms. Send the exact amount for your chosen plan and include your business name in the payment notes so your account can be matched and activated promptly.

- **PayPal** — Send payment to [paypal.me/gsadvertising](https://paypal.me/gsadvertising). Include your business name in the notes field. Standard: $20 · Premium: $30.
- **Cash App** — Send payment to **$GSAdvCon**. Include your business name in the notes field. Standard: $20 · Premium: $30.

## Activate your subscription

<Steps>
  <Step title="Choose your plan">
    Decide between **Standard ($20/month)** and **Premium ($30/month)** based on the features you need. Review the comparison table above if you're unsure which plan fits your goals.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Send your payment">
    Send the correct amount to one of the accepted payment platforms:
    - PayPal: [paypal.me/gsadvertising](https://paypal.me/gsadvertising)
    - Cash App: **$GSAdvCon**
  </Step>
  <Step title="Include your business name in the notes">
    Add your business name in the payment notes or memo field. This is required so the team can match your payment to your account and activate it without delay.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit the payment confirmation form">
    After sending payment, complete the payment confirmation form on the payment page. This notifies the GS Advertising & Connections team that your payment is on its way and provides any additional details needed to activate your account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Wait for activation">
    Your account will be activated within **24 hours** of confirmed payment. You'll receive an email notification once your subscription is live and your listings are enabled.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Subscription terms

Review these terms before completing your purchase:

- Subscriptions provide **30 days of access** beginning on your activation date.
- **No refunds** are issued once a membership has been activated.
- **Billing errors** must be reported to support within **7 days** of payment.
- If your subscription expires, your listings are **hidden from the marketplace but not deleted**. All listing data is preserved and restored automatically when you renew.

<Warning>
  No refunds are issued once a membership has been activated. If you believe there is a billing error — such as being charged the wrong amount or not receiving access after payment — contact support within 7 days of your payment date to have the issue reviewed.
</Warning>
