---

GS Advertising & Connections is a sponsorship marketplace that bridges the gap between local businesses looking to grow their brand and youth or high school sports teams seeking funding. Whether you represent a business ready to invest in your community or a team searching for sponsors, the platform guides you through every step — from creating your profile to finalizing a partnership.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="For Businesses">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Create a free business account">
        Sign up at no cost and build your business profile. Fill in your business name, category, location, and a description that tells teams who you are and what you stand for. A complete profile builds trust and attracts more team applications.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Subscribe to activate your listings">
        Choose a subscription plan to go live in the marketplace. The standard plan is **$20/month** and the Premium plan is **$30/month**. Pay securely via **PayPal** or **Cash App**, then submit your payment confirmation through the platform. Your account is activated within **24 hours**.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Create sponsorship listings">
        Post the sponsorship opportunities you want to offer. For each listing, specify the sponsorship type — **jersey**, **banner**, **tournament**, **social media**, **community event**, or **custom** — along with the sponsorship amount, the benefits you're providing, and any requirements you have for applicant teams.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Review and respond to applications">
        As teams apply to your listings, review each application from your dashboard. **Accept**, **decline**, or **save** applications to revisit later. Respond promptly — businesses with fast response times build stronger reputations in the marketplace.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Build the partnership">
        Once you accept an application, coordinate the sponsorship details directly with the team. Confirm deliverables, timelines, and how your brand will be represented. Your investment goes straight into supporting local youth athletes.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="For Teams">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Create a free team account">
        Sign up at no cost and set up your team profile. Provide your team name, sport, age group, city, state, number of athletes, and a team bio that tells your story. The more detail you include, the more appealing your profile is to potential sponsors.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Browse the marketplace">
        Explore active sponsorship listings from local businesses. Use filters to narrow results by **category**, **location**, and **sponsorship type** so you find opportunities that are the right fit for your team.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Save opportunities">
        Not ready to apply just yet? Save any listing to your dashboard to revisit later. Keep track of opportunities that interest you and apply when the time is right.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Submit a compelling application">
        When you're ready to apply, make your application count. Explain your **team's story**, why the sponsorship is needed, exactly how the funds will be used, and what the business gets in return. Specific, genuine applications stand out and get responses.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Receive and confirm your sponsorship">
        Once a business accepts your application, you'll receive a confirmation through the platform. Reach out directly to coordinate the sponsorship details — including deliverables, timelines, and how you'll represent the sponsor — and get ready to take the field.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

<Note>
  The strongest applications are specific and honest. Instead of a generic request, tell the business exactly how many athletes benefit, what the funds cover (uniforms, travel, tournament fees), and what visibility or recognition the sponsor receives in return. A personal, detailed message dramatically increases your chances of getting a "yes."
</Note>
